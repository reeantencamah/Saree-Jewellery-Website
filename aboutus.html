WHO WE ARE
The journey of Vi Saree & Jewellery began in the year 2017 with the opening of our first wholesale and retail outlet in Mauritius. After the success of this store, Vi Saree & Jewellery launched its online store in 2018, never looking back since then. Today, it is the mauritian's preferred choice for Indian Ethnic Fashion and sets new standards of customer delight every day.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

We are a one-stop webshop offering the widest variety of authentic and trendy ethnic fashion. Our online platform offers sarees, jewellery, contemporary Indo Westerns with an exquisite collection of accessories

OUR MISSION

To provide our customers a truly international online shopping experience, we guarantee superior quality and craftsmanship through our range, supported by swift and secure processes right from purchase to product delivery and beyond

OUR VISION
